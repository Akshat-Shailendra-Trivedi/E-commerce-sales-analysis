## üìù Project Overview
The dataset includes information about **orders, products, customers, and delivery timelines** across various occasions like Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, Holi, Valentine's Day, Birthdays, and Anniversaries. Using Excel, an **interactive dashboard** was created to answer important business questions and optimize sales strategies.  

## üéØ Key Business Questions Answered
1. **Total Revenue:** Overall revenue generated.  
2. **Average Order & Delivery Time:** Time taken from order placement to delivery.  
3. **Monthly Sales Performance:** Sales trends throughout 2023.  
4. **Top Products by Revenue:** Highest revenue-generating products.  
5. **Customer Spending Analysis:** Average spending per customer.  
6. **Sales Performance of Top 5 Products:** Trends for best-selling products.  
7. **Top 10 Cities by Orders:** Cities with highest order volumes.  
8. **Order Quantity vs. Delivery Time:** Effect of large orders on delivery.  
9. **Revenue Comparison Across Occasions:** Revenue generated per occasion.  
10. **Product Popularity by Occasion:** Most popular products for each occasion.

- **Data Visualization:** Charts, slicers, conditional formatting for actionable insights.  
- **Data Cleaning & Manipulation:** Handling missing values, duplicates, and transforming raw data for analysis.  
- **Trend & KPI Analysis:** Monthly sales trends, top product revenue, customer spending patterns, and occasion-based comparisons.
